Property Market Index

Live DemocracyCraft plot prices, built from real closed #realestate auctions - not asking prices.

Methodology

DCT eviction auctions post a nominal "Starting Bid" set by the government - that's a floor, not a market price. The number that matters is the final accepted bid before a post gets tagged Sold. This index reads every closed auction in DemocracyCraft's #realestate forum, pulls out that final price, and rolls it up by zone and district - refreshed automatically every 30 minutes, no stale spreadsheet.
